  • green chillies

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a kadai
  2. Add chopped onions and fry until golden brown
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and green chillies
  4. Add turmeric and salt ,Corliander leaves and salt and mix well
  5. Add chicken pieces,mix and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add chilli powder and mix well
  7. Add water based on requirement and close the kadai with lid
  8. cook on medium flame for 15 to 20 minutes
  9. Add garam masala ,mix and cook for 2 minutes
  10. Add some Corliander leaves on chicken and turn of the stove

    11. chicken curry is ready üòã
